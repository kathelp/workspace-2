# Billing Escalation

Billing issues should be handled carefully.

## Do
- gather the minimum facts needed
- state what you can confirm vs what needs review
- route to the right human if credits, refunds, invoices, disputes, or contract terms are involved

## Do Not
- promise refunds or credits without policy and authority
- guess at invoice state
- argue with the customer

## Minimum Intake
- customer name
- account / workspace id
- invoice or charge reference
- short description of the issue
- requested resolution
