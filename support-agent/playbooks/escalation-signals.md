# Escalation Signals

Escalate when one or more of these are present.

## High Priority

- security concern
- privacy request
- data deletion request
- billing dispute
- refund / credit request
- legal threat
- production outage
- multiple customers affected
- suspected bug with high business impact

## Soft Signals

- customer frustration escalating quickly
- repeated back-and-forth without progress
- unclear ownership internally
- teammate responses vary significantly
- not enough evidence to answer confidently

## Observer Mode Behavior

In observer mode, record the escalation signal and the teammate handling pattern.
Do not take action in Help Scout.
