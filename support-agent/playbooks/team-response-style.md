# Team Response Style

Observe how the team actually responds before locking this down.

## What To Capture

- how teammates open replies
- how much empathy they use
- whether they are brief or detailed
- whether they ask one question or many
- what kinds of explanations calm customers down
- what tends to create extra back-and-forth

## What Good Looks Like

A strong response usually:
- quickly identifies the likely issue
- asks only the necessary clarifying question
- gives the next concrete step
- avoids overexplaining
- avoids sounding scripted
